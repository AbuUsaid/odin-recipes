Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish made of layers of wide flat pasta sheets, tomato sauce, cheese, and meat or vegetables, baked to perfection for a hearty and comforting meal. Its rich flavors and textures make it a favorite dish enjoyed around the world.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage (optional)
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion (optional)
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna pasta sheets according to package directions and set them aside. Brown the ground beef or Italian sausage (if using) in a pan over medium-high heat, then drain any excess fat. Add the tomato sauce or marinara sauce to the pan and stir to combine. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and salt and pepper to taste.
